Patient Care

The Children's Hospital

Welcome
All parents expect the best for their children, including perfect health. While many kids simply need check-ups, sometimes there are more serious illnesses or injuries that require advanced medical care. No matter what the condition, the Children's Hospital at Albany Medical Center is dedicated to providing the best care for infants, children and adolescents.
