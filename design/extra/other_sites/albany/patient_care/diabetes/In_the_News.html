Goodman Diabetes Service In the News

First Goodman Health Expo a Big Success!
On Saturday, April 28, 2007, the first Goodman Diabetes Health Expo was held at the Desmond Hotel and Conference Center in Albany.  145 enthusiastic people attended.  Evaluations were overwhelmingly positive, ranking the day as informative, fun, useful, and inspiring.  "This was the greatest thing!  I think the information that was given was truly life changing." "I thoroughly enjoyed this program of positive thought and actions and so much fun." "The fair was terrific; keep up the great work!"  We do intend to "keep it up"; mark your calendar.
Next year's fair will be held Saturday April 26, 2008.

Eighth Goodman Professional Symposium Held April 27, 2007
The eighth annual Goodman Symposium drew a crowd of over 250 physicians and other health care providers this year.  Participants again evaluated the conference highly positively.
Next year's symposium will be held on Friday April 25, 2008.
