Tuesday June 19, 2007 Insulin Pump Support Group Kicks Off!

This is the first meeting of our new Insulin Pump Support Group!  It will be held in the A1 Endocrinology Clinic Waiting Area from 5 - 6 PM on Tuesday June 19.  Plans for future meetings will be discussed.  It will be facilitated by Beth Nardacci, FNP-C, CDE, BC-ADM and Shannon Thompson MS, PA.  There is no charge.  Call Beth or Shannon at 262-5185 if you have questions.

Tuesday September 11, 2007

Tuesday November 13, 2007
