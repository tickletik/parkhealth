Physical Therapy - Inpatient Services

Albany Medical Center Inpatient Physical Therapy

Utilizing our team of physicians, nurses, therapists, pathologists, psychologists, social workers and case managers each patient receives an individualized program designed to help improve his/her level of function.  The Medical Center's Inpatient Physical Therapy Unit is fully accredited by Medicare and the New York State Department of Health as well as the Joint Commission on the Accreditation of Hospitals.

The most common diagnoses treated in the inpatient unit include patients who have had:

  • Orthopedic surgery
  • Debilitating complex medical illness
  • Cardiac surgery
  • Stroke and other neurologic diseases
  • Trauma
  • Vascular surgery
  • Amputations

Each patient is scheduled for three hours of therapy per day Monday through Friday. Therapy services are also provided on Saturday.

For more information or to refer patients to the Rehabilitation Unit please call the Rehabilitation Department at 518-262-5633.
