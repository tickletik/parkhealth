Patient Care

Outpatient Physical Therapy

Welcome
The Albany Medical Center Outpatient Physical Therapy is committed to providing you with the most outstanding physical therapy experience in the Capital District.  Our goal is to assist you in eliminating your pain and restoring your prior level of activity. We have a dynamic and diverse staff that is committed to delivering effective and professional care to each patient. Our staff uses a combination of traditional techniques and the latest technology available to help you to best achieve your goals.

The Albany Medical Center Advantage
Albany Med's Outpatient Physical Therapy Services are provided by a team of health care professionals that include:

  • Physicians specializing in rehabilitation medicine
  • Nurses
  • Physical therapists
  • Occupational therapists
  • Speech and language pathologists
  • Recreational therapists
  • Rehabilitation psychologists

Offering Innovative Technology and Advanced Procedures
Our outpatient clinic provides numerous rehabilitation services that include:

  • Orthopedic
  • Sports & Athletic Related Injuries
  • Aquatic Therapy
  • Neurological
  • Pediatric
  • Joint Replacement Therapy
  • Workers Compensation and No Fault Injuries

We also accept most insurances. 

Convenient Hours:  Monday through Thursday 7am to 7pm,  Friday 7am to 3:30pm
