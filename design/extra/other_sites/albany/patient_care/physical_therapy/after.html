Patient Care

Physical Therapy - After Care

Here at Albany Med Outpatient Physical Therapy we believe in healthy lifestyle changes. It is vitally important to continue to strengthen and maintain your new condition even after discharge from physical therapy.  That is why we offer an aftercare program upon discharge from formal physical therapy.

The aftercare program allows former patients access to all of the gym equipment in our facility during our normal hours of operation.  Your first two sessions will include personal instruction from Dan Kayajian in order to help you achieve your active lifestyle goals.

There are no long-term commitments or contracts as our fees are month to month.  You will have access to a personal, quiet environment where your therapist is available should you have questions or are in need of more challenging exercises.
