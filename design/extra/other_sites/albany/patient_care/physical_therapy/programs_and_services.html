Patient Care

Physical Therapy - Programs & Services

Physical Therapy can address a number of problems. Below is a list of more common diagnoses that we treat. While we can evaluate your injury you must have a current prescription from a doctor in order to receive treatment.

Your initial evaluation will take from 45 minutes to one hour. Your therapist will conduct a complete evaluation that will examine multiple systems in order to best treat your condition. Subsequent visits usually last from 45 minutes to 60 minutes. Your doctor and therapist will decide how often you may need to come to therapy. Typically most patients come 2 to 3 times per week.

Common Diagnoses/Injuries

  1. Low Back Pain/Strain
  2. Total Joint Replacements
  3. Acute Sprains/Strains of joints
  4. Osteoarthritis
  5. Fibromyalgia
  6. Post-Surgery Diagnoses
  7. Stroke
  8. Spinal Cord Injury
  9. Chronic Pain
  10. Athletic and Sports Injuries
  11. Neurological Impairments / Injuries
  12. Worker's Comp Injuries
  13. No Fault Injuries
  14. Functional Capacity Evaluations (NEW)
