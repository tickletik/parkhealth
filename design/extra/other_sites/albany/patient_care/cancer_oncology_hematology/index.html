Patient Care

Cancer Care - Oncology and Hematology

Welcome
From its adult outpatient oncology and hematology services to the region's only Center for Childhood Cancer and Blood Disorders, Albany Medical Center provides a wide range of services for patients of all ages living with cancer.

The Albany Medical Center Advantage
Albany Medical Center is one of the largest providers of surgical services for cancer patients, providing cutting-edge treatment and technology found nowhere else in the region. Additionally, an entire inpatient unit is devoted to oncology and hematology services.

At Albany Medical Center, physicians and surgeons offer expertise in the diagnosis and treatment of the following:

  • Breast cancer
  • Bladder cancer
  • Bone cancers and cancer-related bone disease
  • Brain tumors and cancers of the spine
  • Cervical cancer
  • Gastrointestinal cancers
  • Head and neck cancers including sinus tumors
  • Leukemia/Lymphoma
  • Lung cancer
  • Ovarian cancer
  • Pancreatic cancer
  • Pituitary tumors
  • Prostate cancer
