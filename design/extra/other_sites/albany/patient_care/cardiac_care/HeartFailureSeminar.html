Patient Care

 

In February 2008 Albany Med hosted an educational seminar for those wanting to learn more about Heart Failure.

Many People have HEART FAILURE and don't know it.

You don't have to be one of them.

With  presentations from an experienced cardiologist and nurse practitioner, this video provides information about the risk and symptoms of heart failure.

In addition, learn how to identify different levels of heart failure and about advanced treatments to help you or a loved one prevent serious heart disease.
