Welcome
From the tiniest hearts to the young at heart, the physicians and professional staff at Albany Medical Center make a difference in the lives of so many. 

The Albany Medical Center Advantage
From open heart surgery for children to the most comprehensive adult heart care in the region,  Albany Med treats nearly 10,000 children and adults for comprehensive heart care annually. 

We offer expertise in the following areas:

  • Angina
  • Arrhythmias
  • Atrial fibrillation
  • Bacterial endocarditis
  • Cardiomyopathy
  • Coronary artery disease
  • Heart attack
  • Heart failure 
  • Heart murmur
  • Heart transplant
  • High blood pressure
  • High cholesterol
  • Mitral valve prolapse

Recognitions and Accreditations

  • Albany Medical Center has been lauded by the American Heart Association/Stroke Association for leading the nation in following evidence-based guidelines for patients with heart failure, coronary artery disease and stroke. By receiving Annual Performance Achievement Awards in all three categories, Albany Med became the only hospital in New York State, and just the second in the nation, to receive this distinction under the American Heart Association/Stroke Association's Get With the Guidelines (GWTG) program. MORE
  • Albany Medical Center has the best (lowest) mortality rate in the state for surgery performed on infants and children with congenital heart defects, according to a report issued by the NYS Department of Health in January 2007.  MORE
