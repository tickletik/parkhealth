Albany Medical Center offers eligible prostate cancer patients an advanced, minimally invasive surgical option - the da Vinci Prostatectomy.  This sophisticated robotic procedure provides a three-dimensional view of vital organs and uses wrist-like instruments to improve surgical precision, dexterity and maneuverability.
