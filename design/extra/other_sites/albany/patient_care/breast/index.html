Patient Care

Breast Center

Welcome
The Breast Center at Albany Med offers a full range of services in breast health and provides patients access to specialty services unavailable anywhere else in the Capital Region. We strive to make it easy for you to receive the best possible care, whether your needs are preventative, routine, chronic, or unexpected.

The Albany Medical Center Advantage
Working together, our team of physicians, nurses, radiologists and other specially-trained staff members, carefully coordinate your care from the first consultation through testing and surgical procedures. 

The Breast Center offers expertise in the following areas:
  • Mammography
  • Diagnostic Mammography
  • Mammotome Biopsy
  • R2  Checker
  • Breast Ultrasound
  • Ultrasound Guided Biopsy
  • Needle Localization 
Dr. Donna Pietrocola consults with patients using advanced diagnostic technology

Offering Innovative Technology and Advanced Procedures
Albany Medical Center offers a comprehensive breast imaging center with state-of-the-art imaging including:

  • Digital Stereotactic Breast Biopsy - Stereotactic breast biopsies represent a new way to precisely and accurately sample tissue from a suspected abnormality found on a mammogram without surgery.  Since the majority of women with an abnormal mammogram do not have breast cancer, but rather one of several benign breast conditions, stereotactic breast biopsy spares most women the discomfort and scarring associated with a surgical breast biopsy.
