Patient Care

Continence Program

Welcome to The Women's Wellness Center of Albany Med -- a state-of-the-art facility designed with the unique physical and emotional needs of women in mind. The Center brings together a highly trained and specialized staff of physicians and nurse practitioners to provide comprehensive individualized health care to women of all ages. The team members work together to provide a wide range of complex and highly individualized services, from basic gynecological care and high-risk pregnancies, to minimally invasive surgical procedures and infertility diagnosis and treatment. It is also comforting to our patients to have direct access through our staff to all the specialists and medical services of the Medical Center -- 24 hours a day, seven days a week.

So whatever your age and whatever your needs, rest assured you'll be catered to by a knowledgeable and professional team in a comfortable and welcoming environment.

A Complete Range of Services:

  • Physical and annual exams
  • General Gynecological care
  • Adolescent gynecology (age 11-18)
  • Menstrual dysfunction evaluation and treatment
  • Perimenopausal and menopause therapy
  • Urogynecology
  • Pelvic floor dysfunction
  • Minimally invasive gynecology surgery - Operating room & office based
  • Obstetrical care
  • Ultrasound referrals and consultations
  • Preconception and prenatal genetics counseling
  • First trimester screening - Nuclear Translucency
  • High risk obstetrics:
         1. Consultations
         2. Level II ultrasound including 3D/4D ultrasounds
         3. Early amniocentesis
         4. CVS (Chrionic Villus Sampling)

Additional Onsite Services:

  • Endocrinology
  • Gastroenterology
  • Neurology
  • Nutrition
  • Smoking Cessation
  • Surgery:
         1. Breast Thyroid Surgery - FNA (Fine Needle Aspiration)
         2. Pediatric Surgeons
         3. Plastic Surgeons
         4. Colo-Rectal Surgeons
