Programs & Services

CONDITIONS

Stress Incontinence
Involuntary loss of urine with physical activity e.g. laugh, cough, sneeze.

Urge Incontinence and/or Urinary Frequency
Voiding 8 or more times per day, 2 or more times at night and/or leakage with strong, sudden urgency.

Urinary Retention
Inability to completely empty the bladder.

Pelvic Support Problems
Prolapse or dropping of pelvic organs (bladder, uterus, bowel, rectum) into or through the vagina.

Other Urinary Conditions
  • Painful bladder
  • Urinary discomfort
  • Vaginal dryness
  • Frequent Urinary Tract Infections
  • Blood in urine
  • Leakage without sensation.


Bladder conditions due to complex medical problems:
MS, Parkinsons Disease, Stroke and Diabetes
