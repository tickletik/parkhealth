Internal Medicine is responsible for the general medical problems and total health of adult patients

Problems Routinely Diagnosed and Treated by Internal Medicine Include:

  • Heart Problems
  • High Cholesterol
  • Hypertension
  • Stomach Problems
Patients should also regularly come in for
  • Yearly Physicals
  • Flu Injections
Complete Bloodwork includes
  • CBC (Complete Blood Count)
  • CMP (Complete Metabolic Profile)
  • Lipid Profile (Serum Cholesterol)
