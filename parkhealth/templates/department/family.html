A Family Physician provides medical care throughout the life of the patient from infancy on.

Because he is intimately familiar with a patients' history, he can give the best possible advice on what medical care is needed. Details which a specialist might not be aware of, are well known by the Family Care physician.

Among the many things a family physician provides:
  • Careful review and renewal of existing treatment programs.
  • Initial evaluation of all injuries, including diagnostic studies such as X-Rays, MRIs, initiation of physical therapy, and pain management
  • Subsequent follow up, and specialty referall when appropriate.
