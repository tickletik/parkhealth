Urology deals with problems of the male and female urinary tract (Kidneys, Ureter, Bladder, and Urethra) as well as the Male Reproductive Organs

These problmes include Urologic Cancers (including Prostate Cancer), Impotence, Urinary Tract Infection, Kidney Stones, Male Infertility, Urinary Incontinence, and some Sexually Transmitted Diseases (STDs).

Urology is classified as a surgical subspecialty. A urologist with advanced qualifications in surgery may be a fellow of the American College of Surgeons (FACs)
